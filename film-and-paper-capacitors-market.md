<p><p>Film and paper capacitors are passive electronic components that store electrical energy. Film capacitors utilize a thin plastic film as a dielectric material, while paper capacitors use paper impregnated with oil or wax. These capacitors are known for their stability, reliability, and ability to withstand high voltages, making them suitable for applications in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and power electronics.</p><p>The Film and Paper Capacitors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.30% during the forecast period. The expansion of electric vehicles and renewable energy systems is also contributing significantly to market growth, as these systems require high-performance capacitors for efficient operation.</p><p>Recent trends indicate a shift towards developing advanced dielectric materials to enhance the performance of film capacitors. Furthermore, the growing emphasis on sustainability is leading manufacturers to explore eco-friendly materials and production processes.
