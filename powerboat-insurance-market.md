<p><strong>The Powerboat Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powerboat insurance market types primarily include Actual Cash Value (ACV) and Agreed Amount Value (AAV). ACV policies reimburse the owner based on the boat's depreciated value at the time of a claim, accounting for wear and tear. Conversely, AAV policies specify a mutually agreed value for the boat, ensuring the owner receives that amount in the event of a total loss, regardless of depreciation. These different approaches cater to diverse owner needs regarding coverage and financial protection.</p></p>
