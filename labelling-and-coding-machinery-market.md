<p><ul><li>Self-adhesive or Pressure Sensitive Labellers</li><li>Shrink Sleeve Labellers</li><li>Glue Based Labellers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The labeling and coding machinery market encompasses several types of labellers, each serving unique purposes. Self-adhesive or pressure-sensitive labellers apply labels that adhere upon contact, offering flexibility and ease of use. Shrink sleeve labellers utilize heat to conform shrink film labels tightly around containers, providing a full 360-degree branding surface. Glue-based labellers utilize adhesive for attaching labels, suitable for various surfaces. Each type facilitates efficient product identification and branding across diverse industries, enhancing packaging aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
