<p><strong>The Smart LED Bulbs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ZigBee</li><li>Wi-Fi</li><li>Bluetooth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart LED Bulbs market consists of three primary types: ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth. ZigBee bulbs use mesh networking for low power consumption and reliable connectivity, ideal for smart home integrations. Wi-Fi bulbs connect directly to home networks, allowing easy control via smartphones without additional hubs, though they may consume more energy. Bluetooth bulbs provide limited range and connectivity but offer easy setup and control directly from devices without the need for internet access, making them user-friendly for localized use.</p></p>
