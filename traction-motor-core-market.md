<p><p>The traction motor core market is primarily divided into two types: permanent magnet motor cores and AC induction motor cores. Permanent magnet motor cores utilize magnets for efficient torque generation, making them compact and energy-efficient, ideal for applications in electric vehicles and trains. AC induction motor cores, on the other hand, rely on electromagnetic induction, offering robustness and lower costs, commonly used in various traction applications. Both types enhance performance and reliability in rail and automotive transport systems.</p></p>
