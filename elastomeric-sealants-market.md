<p><strong>The Elastomeric Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polysulfide (PS)</li><li>Polyurethane (PU)</li><li>Polybutadiene (PB)</li><li>Silicone</li><li>Acrylic</li><li>Silyl Modified Polymer (SMP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elastomeric sealants market encompasses various types, each with distinct properties and applications. Polysulfide (PS) sealants are known for their durability and chemical resistance. Polyurethane (PU) sealants offer high flexibility and adhesion. Polybutadiene (PB) sealants are valued for their low temperature resistance. Silicone sealants provide excellent weatherability and UV resistance. Acrylic sealants are water-based, offering easy application and paintability. Silyl Modified Polymer (SMP) sealants combine the best attributes of silicones and polyurethanes, providing versatility and strong adhesion.</p></p>
