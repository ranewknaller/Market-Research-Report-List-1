<p><p>The electronic faucets market is divided into two main types: touchless and touched electronic faucets. Touchless electronic faucets operate using sensors that detect hand movement, allowing users to activate water flow without physical contact, promoting hygiene and water conservation. In contrast, touched electronic faucets require users to physically touch a control surface to initiate water flow, combining convenience with traditional use. Both types aim to enhance water efficiency and user experience in residential, commercial, and public settings.</p></p>
