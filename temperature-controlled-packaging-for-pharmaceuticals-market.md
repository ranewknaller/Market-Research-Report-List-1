<p><strong>The Temperature Controlled Packaging for Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Temperature Controlled System</li><li>Passive Temperature Controlled System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Temperature Controlled Packaging in the pharmaceutical market encompasses two main types: Active and Passive Temperature Controlled Systems. Active systems utilize energy sources like refrigerators or battery-powered units to maintain specific temperatures, ensuring precise control during transportation. In contrast, Passive systems rely on insulation and phase change materials to protect products from temperature fluctuations without active energy input. Both systems are crucial for preserving the integrity of temperature-sensitive pharmaceuticals, ensuring their efficacy and safety during storage and distribution.</p></p>
