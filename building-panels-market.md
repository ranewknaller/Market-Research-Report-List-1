<p><p>The building panels market comprises several types, each serving unique functions. Concrete panels offer durability and thermal mass for strength and energy efficiency. Vacuum insulated panels (VIP) provide superior insulation with minimal thickness, ideal for space-constrained applications. Structural insulated panels (SIP) combine insulation and structural support, enhancing energy efficiency in construction. Wood panels are renewable and versatile, used for aesthetics and insulation. Together, these panel types cater to diverse construction needs, promoting sustainability and energy efficiency.</p></p>
