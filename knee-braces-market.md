<p><p>The knee braces market is categorized into two primary types: prophylactic and functional braces. In contrast, functional braces are intended for individuals recovering from knee injuries or surgeries, offering support to maintain knee function and facilitate rehabilitation. Both types serve distinct purposes, addressing the needs of athletes and patients in different phases of injury prevention and recovery.</p></p>
