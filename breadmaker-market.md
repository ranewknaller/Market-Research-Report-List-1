<p><p>The breadmaker market is primarily divided into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic breadmakers handle the entire baking process, from mixing ingredients to baking and cooling, requiring minimal user intervention; they often feature preset programs for various bread types. In contrast, semi-automatic breadmakers require some manual input, such as ingredient measurement or kneading, allowing users more control over the baking process. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, from convenience to hands-on baking experiences.</p></p>
