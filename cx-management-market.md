<p><p>CX Management solutions can be categorized into two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise systems are installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data, security, and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and IT resources for maintenance. In contrast, Cloud-Based solutions are hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and lower initial costs. These solutions enable easier access and updates, making them ideal for organizations looking for rapid deployment and lower administrative burdens.</p></p>
