<p><p>The industrial catalysts market comprises various types, each serving distinct functions in chemical processes. Polyolefin catalysts facilitate the production of polyolefins, crucial for plastics manufacturing. Supported metal catalysts enhance reaction efficiency in chemical transformations by providing active metal sites on a support material. Zeolite catalysts, with their porous structures, are essential in petrochemical refining and converting hydrocarbons, offering selectivity and stability. Together, these catalysts significantly improve reaction rates and product yields across multiple industrial applications.</p></p>
