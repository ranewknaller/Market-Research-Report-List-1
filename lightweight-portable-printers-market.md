<p><p>The lightweight portable printers market is primarily categorized into two types: direct thermal printers and thermal transfer printers. </p><p>Direct thermal printers use heat-sensitive paper to create images, ideal for short-lived labels and shipping tags. They are compact, making them convenient for mobile use. In contrast, thermal transfer printers utilize a ribbon to produce durable, high-quality prints on various materials, suitable for longer-lasting applications like product labeling. Both types cater to different printing needs in sectors such as retail, logistics, and healthcare.</p></p>
