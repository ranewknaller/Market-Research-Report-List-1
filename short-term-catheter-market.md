<p><p>The short-term catheter market includes three primary types: latex catheters, PVC catheters, and silicone catheters. Latex catheters are known for their elasticity and flexibility but can cause allergic reactions in some patients. PVC catheters are cost-effective and provide good biocompatibility, making them widely used for short-term applications. Silicone catheters are biocompatible, non-reactive, and ideal for long-term use, as they reduce the risk of friction and irritation. Each type serves specific medical needs and patient considerations.</p></p>
