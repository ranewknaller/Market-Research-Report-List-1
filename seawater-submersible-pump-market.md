<p><ul><li>Single Stage Submersible Pump</li><li>Multi Stage Submersible Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seawater submersible pump market is primarily categorized into two types: single-stage and multi-stage submersible pumps. Single-stage submersible pumps feature one impeller, making them ideal for applications requiring lower head and higher flow rates, such as desalination. In contrast, multi-stage submersible pumps consist of multiple impellers, enabling them to achieve higher pressures and lift water from greater depths, making them suitable for industrial and municipal applications where elevated water pressures are essential.</p></p>
