<p><p>Insecticidal seed treatment encompasses three primary market types: seed dressing, seed coating, and seed pelleting. Seed dressing involves applying insecticides directly to seeds to protect against pests. Seed coating refers to a more extensive application that enhances adhesion and protection, often including fungicides and nutrients. Seed pelleting transforms seeds into uniform, larger pellets that ease handling and planting while offering pest protection. These treatments improve seed health and agricultural productivity by minimizing pest-related losses.</p></p>
