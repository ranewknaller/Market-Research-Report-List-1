<p><ul><li>Semiconductor Underfills</li><li>Board Level Underfills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underfill market is primarily divided into two main types: semiconductor underfills and board-level underfills. Semiconductor underfills refer to epoxy materials applied between semiconductor chips and substrates to enhance mechanical strength and thermal performance, essential for high-density packaging. Board-level underfills, on the other hand, are used to secure surface-mounted devices (SMDs) on printed circuit boards (PCBs), providing protection against thermal cycling and mechanical stress, thus improving reliability and longevity of electronic assemblies.</p></p>
