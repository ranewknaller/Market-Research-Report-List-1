<p><p>The VoIP softphone market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based softphones operate over the internet, allowing users to access communication tools from any device with connectivity. This model offers scalability and reduced maintenance costs. In contrast, on-premises softphones are installed directly on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over security and system customizations. Both types enhance business communication but cater to different needs based on budget, IT resources, and operational preferences.</p></p>
