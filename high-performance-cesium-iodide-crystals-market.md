<p><p>The high-performance cesium iodide crystals market is segmented into three main types: CsI(Tl), CsI(Na), and pure CsI. CsI(Tl) crystals, doped with thallium, are widely used in medical imaging and radiation detection due to their high light yield and efficiency. CsI(Na), doped with sodium, offers superior performance in certain applications like scintillation detectors. Pure CsI crystals, free from dopants, are utilized in applications requiring high transmission and stability, such as in electronics and radiation shielding.</p></p>
