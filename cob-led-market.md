<p><p>The COB LED market encompasses two primary types: Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLEDs) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diodes (ILEDs). OLEDs utilize organic compounds to emit light, offering flexibility, high efficiency, and superior color quality, which makes them ideal for displays and lighting applications. In contrast, ILEDs are based on inorganic materials, providing durability, higher brightness, and longevity, making them suitable for general lighting and commercial uses. Both types cater to diverse applications in the growing LED market.</p></p>
