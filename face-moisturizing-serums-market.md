<p><p>The face moisturizing serums market can be divided into two main types: water-based and oil-based formulations. Water-based serums typically feature lightweight, hydrating ingredients that absorb quickly, making them ideal for oily or combination skin. They often include humectants like hyaluronic acid to attract moisture. In contrast, oil-based serums provide rich hydration and nourishment, suitable for dry or mature skin types. These formulations usually contain oils rich in fatty acids, helping to lock in moisture and improve skin texture and elasticity.</p></p>
