<p><strong>The Micro SD Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SD (8M-2G)</li><li>SDHC (2G-32G)</li><li>SDXC (32G-400G)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro SD cards are categorized into three main types based on storage capacity. Standard SD cards range from 8MB to 2GB, suitable for basic file storage. SDHC (Secure Digital High Capacity) cards offer a range from 2GB to 32GB, ideal for smartphones and cameras requiring moderate storage. SDXC (Secure Digital eXtended Capacity) cards extend from 32GB to 400GB, allowing high-resolution content storage and applications demanding larger space, making them perfect for advanced devices and heavy multimedia use.</p></p>
