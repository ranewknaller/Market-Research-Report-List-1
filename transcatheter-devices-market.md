<p><p>The transcatheter devices market includes various minimally invasive procedures for heart valve replacement and repair. TAVR (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Replacement) and TAVI (Transcatheter Aortic Valve Implantation) are used interchangeably to address aortic stenosis. TMVR (Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair) focuses on repairing the mitral valve, while TMVI (Transcatheter Mitral Valve Implantation) involves the placement of a new valve. These technologies reduce surgical risks and recovery times, making them increasingly popular for patients with heart valve diseases.</p></p>
