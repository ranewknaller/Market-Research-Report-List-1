<p><p>Automotive Active Grille Shutter Systems enhance vehicle aerodynamics and engine efficiency by regulating airflow through the grille. The market is divided into two main types: horizontal vanes and vertical vanes. Horizontal vanes are oriented parallel to the ground, optimizing airflow during highway driving and improving aerodynamic performance. Vertical vanes, on the other hand, are positioned upright, allowing for better cooling in stop-and-go conditions. Both types contribute to reduced drag and improved fuel economy while adapting to varying driving conditions.</p></p>
