<p><p>PLM (Product Lifecycle Management) and engineering software markets are categorized into three main types: on-premises, on-demand, and cloud-based. On-premises solutions require local installation and management, offering high control but demanding significant IT resources. On-demand platforms, also known as SaaS (Software as a Service), provide software via the internet, allowing for easy access and scalability without heavy infrastructure investments. Cloud-based solutions combine the flexibility of on-demand access with the power of centralized data storage, facilitating collaboration and continuous updates.</p></p>
