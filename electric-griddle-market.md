<p><p>The electric griddle market features two main types: manual control and thermostatic control. Manual control griddles allow users to adjust temperature settings manually, offering simplicity and direct control over cooking heat. In contrast, thermostatic control griddles automatically regulate temperature, ensuring consistent heat during cooking and reducing the risk of overheating. Each type caters to different user preferences; manual control appeals to those who enjoy hands-on cooking, while thermostatic control is ideal for those seeking convenience and precision.</p></p>
