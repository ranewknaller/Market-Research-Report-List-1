<p><p>A Plate and Frame Filter Press is a type of mechanical separation equipment used widely in industries such as food and beverage, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and wastewater treatment. It operates by pressing plates covered with filter cloths to create chambers where slurry is introduced. The pressure forces the liquid to pass through the filter media, leaving behind solid particles. This technology is favored for its efficiency in solid-liquid separation, ease of operation, and ability to handle varying feed compositions.</p><p>The Plate and Frame Filter Press Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period.
