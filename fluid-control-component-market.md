<p><p>The Fluid Control Component Market encompasses various types of valves essential for regulating fluid flow in systems. Solenoid valves use electromagnetic coils to control flow electronically, offering rapid response. Air-operated valves utilize compressed air for operation, providing reliable control in automated processes. Check valves are designed to prevent backflow, ensuring fluid moves in one direction. Other fluid control components may include gate valves, ball valves, and pressure relief valves, each serving specific roles in enhancing system efficiency and safety.</p></p>
